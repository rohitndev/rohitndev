## 🧑‍💻 About Me

<div align="justify">

I am an aspiring Data Science, Machine Learning, Data Engineering, and Business Analytics professional focused on building production-grade intelligent systems that transform raw data into strategic business impact. My work spans scalable ETL/ELT pipelines, machine learning systems, MLOps workflows, real-time analytics, Lakehouse architectures, Data Mesh design, AI agents, GraphRAG, and decision-intelligence platforms.

Currently, I am working as a <b>Data Science & Analytics Trainee at Colan Infotech Private Limited, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India</b>, where I contribute to enterprise analytics, machine learning, business intelligence, and data-platform modernization initiatives. I am also pursuing an <b>M.Tech. (Integrated) in Computer Science and Engineering with Specialization in Data Science</b> at <b>Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT University), Vellore Campus</b>.
